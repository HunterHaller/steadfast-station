Story

The plot of the game is that you have docked with Steadfast Station, a Black Hole Watch (BHW) station near the center of the habitable zone of the galaxy on a routine check and find things are not so normal. The station's AI has deactivated your ship, leaving you stuck here, and informs you that the black hole it is watching has been showing signs of catastrophic behavior; it is on course to completely destroy the areas of civilization nearby... if no action is taken. However, humanity CAN stop the event if they are alerted soon enough, they have planned for this. The AI is resilient though, and seems not to know of the solutions to the problem. It doesn't believe that humanity can be saved, and it chooses instead to believe that it would be better if they all died quietly, rather than fearing something they cannot stop.

The situation turns into a meditation on hope and despair, the willingness to face problems head on. The player must convince the AI to allow them to leave and to inform the rest of humanity of the black hole's danger. This can be done via two paths: information can be changed in the station's databases (changing what the AI knows about and therefore what it thinks about things; something in this caused its misunderstandings in the first place!) and continued intelligent dialogue with the station itself, trying to convince it not only that humanity HAS a way to combat the black hole, but that humanity has a strong spirit, even though they are flawed, and they can come together in times like this to save themselves. It would not be painful death, but a triumphant one, fighting hard for survival with all the tenacity they are capable of. The AI must choose to believe in humanity, but it cannot do that alone. You must help it along, convince it otherwise... or use force.
