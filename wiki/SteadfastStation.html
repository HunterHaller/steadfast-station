Steadfast Station

Steadfast Station is a game in which players inhabit the role of an interstellar technician who remotely interfaces with malfunctioning stations throughout the galaxy via their computational device to repair them and keep them running smoothly. The player's computer becomes the computer of the character as the game runs simply in their own browser, transporting them to that place in a way.
