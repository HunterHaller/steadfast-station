Black Hole Watch

Black Hole Watch (BHW) is the duty of observing black holes near areas inhabited by humans. This observation is generally conducted by unmanned, AI-driven stations, simply labelled BHW Stations.

The BHW directive was put in place by the Revulend Corporation as a response to a black hole that grew to a dangerous size more quickly than anyone could have anticipated and ended up swallowing Corporation-owned station world whole in an event known as the Stridesmark Disaster.
