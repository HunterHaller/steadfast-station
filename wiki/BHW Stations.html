BHW Stations

Unmanned, AI-driven space stations whose task is to observe black holes for dangerous activity as part of the Black Hole Watch directive. They often accompany World Stations, as those are generally located near large black holes.

History

After the unparallaled cost of the Stridesmark Disaster (both in terms of life and finances), the Revulend Corporation put the Black Hole Watch directive into place in order to prevent future disasters. The stations' primary task is to watch for unwarranted black hole expansion (the event which triggered the Disaster) so that the station inhabitants may be warned in time

Technicians and Hacking

All stations need routine maintenance that is done by galaxy-roaming technicians. The technician docks with the station and links to it via a personal computation device and proceeds to make sure all softare and hardware is running correctly.

If major changes must be made to the software, access is possible and intended for The Technician. Technicians with the correct clearance level have full read and write access to all data stored in station databanks as well as AI software packages.
