Stridesmark Disaster

The Stridesmark Disaster was the first instance of a black hole erupting into enormous size and swallowing the stationworld of Stridesmark whole with no survivors. Due to the black hole's proximity to the station at the time, no communication was possible with the station, and no ships were able to escape. The disaster resulted in trillions of Credits worth of loss for the Revulend Corporation and sparked their initiative to enact the Black Hole Watch directive.
